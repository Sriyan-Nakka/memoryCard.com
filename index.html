How to Play

Welcome to Memory Card! A card game where you have to select the same pair of cards.

Playing is simple. There are 6 pairs AKA 12 cards, from which you take turns selecting 2 cards at a time. If the selected 2 cards are pairs AKA have the same shape, you get a point. If you find all the pairs, you win! Hope you like it!
